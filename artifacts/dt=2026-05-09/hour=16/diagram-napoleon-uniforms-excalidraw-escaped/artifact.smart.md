## Reading the sketch

- **Blue infantry blocks** represent the backbone of the army.
- **Red Guard block** marks prestige and battlefield reserve.
- **Warm cavalry circles** show mounted arms with distinct visual identities.
- **Artillery** is separated because its uniform marked a specialized technical branch.
